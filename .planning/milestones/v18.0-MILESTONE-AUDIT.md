## Integration Check

**14/14 connections verified.** 2 bugs found and fixed during audit:

### Bugs Fixed

**BUG-1 (Critical, fixed):** Caddy nativeApp cookie gate checked `livinity_token` — a cookie that was never set. Changed to `LIVINITY_SESSION`. Without this fix, the desktop viewer page was unreachable.

**BUG-2 (High, fixed):** `applyCaddyConfig()` in caddy.ts was called without nativeApps from domain/routes.ts. Domain reconfiguration erased the `pc.{domain}` Caddy block. Now forwards NATIVE_APP_CONFIGS.
