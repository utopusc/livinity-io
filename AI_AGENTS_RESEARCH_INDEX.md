## Key Findings Summary

### Winners (Production-Ready, Self-Hosted Friendly)
1. **CrewAI** — Industry standard for multi-agent orchestration
2. **n8n** — Best for business process automation + integrations
3. **Langflow** — Best for visual AI workflows, non-technical users
4. **Skyvern** — Best for RPA and browser automation
5. **MCP** — Industry de-facto for tool integration

### Honorable Mentions
- **Nextcloud AI** — Complementary to Livinity (files + collab)
- **Venn.ai** — Good pattern for safe AI access to business systems
- **Relay.app** — Good pattern for human-in-the-loop approvals

### Watch Out For
- **OpenClaw** — Powerful but serious security concerns (broad permissions)
- **Zapier** — Cloud-only (no self-hosting)
- **Claude Cowork** — Closed source, cloud only (but shows direction)
